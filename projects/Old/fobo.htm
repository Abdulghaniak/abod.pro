FOBO

Tire Pressure Monitoring System

Role

Android Developer & Graphic Designer

Overview

FOBO Tire is an advanced Wireless Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) using latest Bluetooth 4.0 that works directly with your smartphone or with an intelligent In-Car monitoring unit that works on its own, FOBO Tire monitors up to 350 kPa(50 psi) and give you an alert in the absence of a smartphone.


Tech

My part was to create an Android Application that read the RSSI of the BLE sensors to receive the signal strength and establish the connection for monitoring the tire pressure.
