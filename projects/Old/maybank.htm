Maybank Website

Role

Front-End Developer & Web Designer

Technologies

HTML5, Css3, JavaScript, Photoshop

Overview

Maybank is the fourth largest bank in South East Asia, with total assets of more than RM722 billion. Maybank Group is the leading financial services provider in Malaysia catering to the needs of consumers, investors, entrepreneurs, non-profit organisations and corporations. The Group, which has expanded internationally, has the largest network among Malaysian banks of over 2,400 branches and offices in 20 countries, employing over 44,000 Maybankers and serving over 22 million customers. It is the only regional bank with a presence in all 10 ASEAN countries.

Objective

With over 3m unique visitors, Maybank2U is the most popular Malaysian website. However, its design is not that great. It looks like a site designed in the 90s.

The website involved a lot of re-designing and UI/UX fixes to incorporate a modern look, responsive layouts and better UX for mobile devices. As the website and its content was really outdated I started the project listing the unnecessary content and making it simpler and shorter, to catch the user attention to the main tabs. And I pitched several different approaches that echo a refreshed look and feel for the website. Those changes could push the website from just being usable to being delightful.
