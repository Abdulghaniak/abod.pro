FOBO

Tire Pressure Monitoring System

Objective

Graphic Design & Native Android Development

Overview

FOBO Tire is an advanced Wireless Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) using latest Bluetooth 4.0 that works directly with the user's smartphone or with an intelligent In-Carmonitoring unit that works on its own, FOBO Tire monitors up to 350 kPa(50 psi) and give the user an alert in the absence of a smartphone.


Objective

I have been involved in Fobo Tires project to create an Android Application that read the RSSI of the BLE sensors to receive the signal strength and establish the connection for monitoring the tire pressure.
